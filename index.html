Courtroom Assistance Program

CAP provides a free attorney and free assistance to parents and guardians who have a qualified legal situation involving a child. View our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) for more information.

If you are having issues involving visitation, custody, possession or access to your child, CAP may be able to help. CAP specializes in helping fathers, mothers, grandparents and guardians resolve these types of situations.
