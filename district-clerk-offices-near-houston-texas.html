District Clerk Offices Near Houston, Texas

Find contact information and locations for District Clerk offices in the Houston, Texas area to help you with your family law case filings and documentation.

Harris County District Clerk

The Harris County District Clerk's office handles family law cases, including divorces, child custody, and child support cases in Houston and Harris County.

Main Office

Address: 201 Caroline St, Houston, TX 77002

Phone: (832) 927-5800

Hours: Monday - Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Fort Bend County District Clerk

The Fort Bend County District Clerk serves families in the Fort Bend County area, southwest of Houston.

Main Office

Address: 301 Jackson St, Richmond, TX 77469

Phone: (281) 341-3718

Hours: Monday - Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Montgomery County District Clerk

Montgomery County serves families in the northern Houston area, including The Woodlands, Conroe, and surrounding communities.

Main Office

Address: 301 N Thompson St, Suite 102, Conroe, TX 77301

Phone: (936) 539-7897

Hours: Monday - Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Brazoria County District Clerk

Brazoria County serves families south of Houston, including Pearland, Alvin, and Lake Jackson.

Main Office

Address: 111 E Locust St, Suite 200, Angleton, TX 77515

Phone: (979) 864-1219

Hours: Monday - Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Important Filing Information

When filing family law documents, it's important to know which county has jurisdiction over your case. Generally, cases are filed in the county where the respondent resides or where the family last lived together.
