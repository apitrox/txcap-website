Abbott Signs Sweeping Child Welfare Overhaul into Law

Governor Abbott signs comprehensive child welfare reform legislation affecting Texas families.

Major Child Welfare Reform in Texas

Governor Greg Abbott has signed into law significant reforms to Texas's child welfare system, representing one of the most comprehensive overhauls in recent history.

Key Provisions of the Reform

The legislation includes provisions designed to improve outcomes for children in the foster care system, enhance support for foster families, and strengthen oversight of Child Protective Services (CPS).

Impact on Texas Families

These reforms aim to ensure that children receive better care and that families involved in the child welfare system have access to necessary resources and support.

Stay Informed

Families affected by the child welfare system should stay informed about how these changes may impact their cases and rights.
